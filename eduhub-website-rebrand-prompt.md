# Eduhub — Website Rebrand Brief (Direction: "The Scholar")

You are redesigning the Eduhub website to match a new, finalized brand identity.
Eduhub is a B2B school-services company in Istanbul. From competitions and trips to
uniforms, gifts, and graduation — Eduhub plans, supplies, and runs it, in Arabic and
English. The primary audience is **school administrators and principals** at
international schools. The brand must feel **trustworthy, established, and credible** —
a long-term partner, not a vendor — with quiet warmth.

Apply the design system below faithfully. Do not invent new colors, fonts, or logo
treatments. When in doubt, choose the more restrained, more "institutional" option.

---

## 1. Brand voice & feel
- **Personality:** trustworthy, established, organized, calm, warm. Think "respected
  institution," not "startup."
- **Tone of copy:** clear, confident, reassuring. Short declarative sentences. Lead with
  outcomes for the school ("handled," "one team," "from the first bell to graduation").
- **Avoid:** hype, exclamation marks, playful slang, childish/"crayon" school clichés,
  heavy gradients, emoji, stock-photo overload.

## 2. Logo
- Use the provided **Scholar** logo: a graduation-cap crest inside a thin double-ring
  seal (navy ring + brass inner ring), paired with a serif "Eduhub" wordmark.
- Provided files:
  - `eduhub-scholar-primary-stacked.png` — primary, crest above wordmark
  - `eduhub-scholar-horizontal.png` — header/nav use
  - `eduhub-scholar-reverse-navy.png` — for dark/navy backgrounds
  - `eduhub-scholar-arabic.png` — Arabic lockup
  - `eduhub-scholar-mark-color.png` / `-reverse.png` / `-mono-navy.png` — crest only (favicon, app icon, avatars)
- **Header:** horizontal lockup, navy on light. **Footer:** reverse lockup on navy.
- **Favicon / app icon:** the crest mark only.
- Maintain clear space of at least the crest's height around the logo. Never stretch,
  recolor, re-skin, or replace the serif wordmark font. Minimum full-lockup width ~90px;
  below that use the crest mark alone.

## 3. Color palette
| Token        | Hex       | Use |
|--------------|-----------|-----|
| Harbor Navy  | `#14355A` | Primary — headings, footer, hero, logo |
| Eduhub Blue  | `#1A4D8F` | Buttons, links, primary actions |
| Sky          | `#2E86C7` | Secondary accents, hovers, highlights |
| Sky Light    | `#5FB0E6` | Subtle accents, the "hub" emphasis |
| Mist         | `#DCEBF7` | Tinted section backgrounds, cards |
| Brass        | `#D9A441` | **Accent only** — small details, underlines, icon ticks, never large fills |
| Paper        | `#F7F5EF` | Warm off-white page/section background |
| Ink          | `#1C2A36` | Body text |
| Slate        | `#5C6B78` | Secondary/muted text |

**Color ratio:** ~70% navy + neutrals (Paper/white), ~25% blues, ~5% brass.
Brass is a seasoning — use it for accent lines, small marks, and the cap-tassel detail.
Never use brass as a button fill or large background.

## 4. Typography
- **Headings / display:** **Spectral** (serif), weights 400–600. Used for H1–H3,
  hero, section titles. Slight negative letter-spacing on large sizes (~-0.01em).
- **Body / UI:** **Hanken Grotesk** (sans), weights 400/600/800. Body copy, nav,
  buttons, labels, captions.
- **Arabic:** **IBM Plex Sans Arabic**, weights 400–600. Apply `dir="rtl"` to Arabic
  blocks; mirror layouts for the Arabic site.
- Load all three from Google Fonts. Body text minimum 16px; never below 14px.

## 5. Layout & components
- **Grid:** generous whitespace, max content width ~1200px, comfortable line length.
  Calm, editorial rhythm — this is an institution, not a busy marketplace.
- **Sections:** alternate white / Paper (`#F7F5EF`) backgrounds; use one navy section
  for emphasis (hero or CTA band) with the reverse logo.
- **Buttons:** primary = solid `#1A4D8F`, white text, pill or 8px radius, Hanken
  Grotesk 600. Secondary = navy outline on transparent. Hover shifts to `#2E86C7`.
- **Cards:** white, 14–18px radius, 1px `#EAE6DC` border, soft low shadow. Optional
  thin brass top-accent line for featured cards.
- **Service tiles** (Competitions, Trips, Uniforms, Gifts, Graduation, Activities):
  navy serif title, slate description, a small simple geometric icon in navy with a
  brass detail. Keep icons minimal and consistent — no detailed illustrations.
- **Imagery:** real photography of schools, students, uniforms and events; apply a
  subtle navy duotone or a navy overlay for cohesion. Use placeholders where photos
  are missing — never AI-illustrate.
- **Forms / CTAs:** the key conversion is "Book a school visit" / "Request a proposal."
  Make these prominent, calm, and reassuring.

## 6. Bilingual
- Full English + Arabic versions. English is primary; provide a clear EN/عربى switch
  in the header. Arabic pages are fully RTL with mirrored layout and Arabic lockup.
- Keep the visual system identical across both languages — same colors, spacing,
  components — so it reads as one brand.

## 7. Page structure (suggested)
1. **Header** — horizontal logo, nav (Services, Schools, About, Contact), language
   switch, primary "Book a visit" button.
2. **Hero** — navy background, reverse logo or large Spectral headline
   ("From the first bell to graduation day."), short subhead, primary CTA, one strong
   school photo.
3. **What we do** — the six services as tiles.
4. **Why schools trust Eduhub** — 3–4 reassurance points (one team, end-to-end,
   bilingual, Istanbul-based), calm and factual.
5. **How it works** — simple 3-step process.
6. **Proof** — partner schools / testimonials (placeholders if needed).
7. **CTA band** — navy, brass accent, "Request a proposal."
8. **Footer** — reverse logo on navy, contact, language switch, quick links.

## 8. Acceptance checklist
- [ ] Spectral for headings, Hanken Grotesk for body, IBM Plex Sans Arabic for Arabic.
- [ ] Exact palette hexes; brass used only as a small accent (~5%).
- [ ] Scholar crest logo in header (horizontal) and footer (reverse on navy).
- [ ] Crest-only mark as favicon.
- [ ] Full RTL Arabic version with language switch.
- [ ] No gradients-as-decoration, no emoji, no childish styling, no stretched/recolored logo.
- [ ] Calm, institutional, trustworthy feel throughout.
